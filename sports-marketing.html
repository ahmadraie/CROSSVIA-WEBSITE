SPORTS MARKETING AND SPONSORSHIP

CROSSVIA leverages its extensive network and industry insights to craft strategic sports marketing and sponsorship campaigns that deliver measurable results.

Brand Integration

Brand Positioning and Messaging

Crafting compelling brand narratives and positioning strategies that resonate with the target audience, ensuring consistent and effective messaging across all event platforms and media channels.

Brand Integration into Sports Events

Strategically placing and integrating brands into live sports events, ensuring they resonate with audiences while aligning with the event's theme and values for maximum visibility and impact.

Digital and Social Media Integration

Leveraging digital platforms and social media channels to integrate brands into real-time content, campaigns, and fan engagement activities, enhancing online presence and audience interaction.

Sponsorship Acquisition

Sponsorship Programs

Developing tailored sponsorship programs that connect brands with the right sports properties, creating meaningful engagements that drive value for both sponsors and their target audience.

Event Sponsorships and Partnerships

Managing the integration of brands into high-profile sports events, tournaments, and leagues, ensuring cohesive messaging and maximum ROI through tailored sponsorship strategies.

Strategic Brand Partnerships

Facilitating long-term strategic partnerships between brands and sports organizations, fostering collaborative opportunities that go beyond one-time sponsorships for sustained visibility and engagement.

Fan Engagement Programs

Interactive Digital Campaigns

Creating engaging online experiences, from live-streamed events to interactive social media contests, where fans can participate and directly engage with their favorite sports brands and teams. In addition to using VR/AR technologies that allows fans to engage deeper with teams, players and events virtually.

Event-Based Engagement

Creating memorable fan experiences at live sports events, including product sampling, and fan-focused contests that generate excitement and deepen brand connections.

Content Creation and Collaboration

Automated Broadcasting

Utilizing AI and automation for live sports broadcasting and creating personalized highlights.

Creative Content Development

Producing engaging content that empowers brand recognition through video, storytelling, and digital content.

Media Coverage Alignment

Ensuring brand visibility through strategic media placements, including TV broadcasts, social media, and digital platforms to amplifying brand exposure.

Collaborations with Influencers and Athletes

Identifying and partnering with athletes and influencers who align with brand values, creating authentic collaborations that amplify brand messaging and expand reach to targeted audiences for events and brands.
