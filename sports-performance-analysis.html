ANALYSIS

CROSSVIA offers comprehensive sports performance analysis services to optimize athlete training programs and team strategies.

Video Analysis

Comprehensive Game Footage Analysis

Reviewing game footage to evaluate team dynamics, individual player performance, and overall strategy, identifying strengths, weaknesses, and areas for tactical improvement.

Player Performance Breakdown

Using advanced video techniques to break down individual player movements, positioning, and decision-making during games, enabling targeted coaching and skill refinement.

Tactical Analysis

Analyzing game tactics, formations, and in-game strategies to assess their effectiveness, identify opportunities for tactical adjustments, and provide recommendations for improved team performance.

Biomechanical Analysis

Player Movement and Technique Analysis

Using motion tracking technology and video analysis tools to evaluate player movements, biomechanics, and technical execution, helping to refine skills and techniques for better efficiency and effectiveness.

Kinematic and Kinetic Analysis

Detailed analysis of joint angles, muscle activation, and force generation to evaluate an athlete's technique, form, and overall efficiency, ensuring peak performance while minimizing injury.

Video and Motion Capture Analysis

Implementing high-speed video capture and motion analysis systems to evaluate techniques in real-time, enabling coaches and athletes to make immediate adjustments.

Recovery and Rehabilitation Monitoring

Using biomechanical analysis to track progress during recovery phases, ensuring that athletes are rehabilitating properly and regaining optimal movement patterns.

Predictive Analytics

Player Performance Metrics
