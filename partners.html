Our Partners

At CROSSVIA, we seek partnerships with distinguished organizations that share our dedication to excellence in the sports industry. Our potential partners include industry leaders, innovators, and key players across various sectors, each bringing unique expertise and perspectives to enhance our offerings. By combining our collective strengths, we deliver tailored solutions that drive success, enhance performance, and unlock new opportunities in the ever-evolving sports landscape. Explore our strategic partnerships and discover how our combined expertise can help you achieve your goals and create a lasting impact on the sports industry.

IMG

IMG Consulting is a division of IMG, a global leader in sports, events, media, and fashion. They offer a wide range of services designed to enhance the performance and marketability of sports entities. IMG Consulting provides strategic planning, sponsorship acquisition, brand development, Hospitality, event management, and media rights consulting.

Premier Sport Psychology

a leading provider of sports psychology services based in Minnesota, USA. The company offers a wide range of mental performance services to athletes, coaches, and organizations. Their offerings include mental skills training, performance enhancement techniques, leadership development, team dynamics, and career transition support. They work with individuals and teams.

Octagon

Octagon is a leading global sports and entertainment marketing agency. They offer consulting services to help clients maximize their impact in the sports industry. Octagon provides sponsorship and marketing consulting, athlete and talent management, event management, and digital and social media strategy.

Optimum Nutrition

The company is involved in various regional fitness initiatives and events such as the Dubai Fitness Challenge and Dubai Active Show. Optimum Nutrition aims to raise awareness about nutrition, health, and sports, providing expert advice to athletes and fitness enthusiasts.

Gatorade Sports Science Institute

GSSI conducts cutting-edge research on sports nutrition and offers consulting services based on its findings. Its services focus on hydration, energy intake, and recovery strategies for athletes. GSSI also provides individual consultations, team workshops, and educational resources on sports nutrition. In addition, its line of service also includes training and workshops in sports science.

Wasserman

Wasserman is a global sports marketing and talent management company that represents athletes, coaches, and broadcasters across various sports. It offers Athlete management, marketing and endorsement deals, social media strategy, financial planning, brand building, and event management.

Catapult Sports

We are a global company that works with top leagues, teams, and organizations to help them unleash their potential. It is basically a cloud-based platform for managing and analyzing team performance data.

Infront Sports & Media

is a major player in sports marketing and media rights management. It manages media rights, sponsorships, and event production for various sports. It has worked with international sports federations, leagues, and major brands
