Strategic Partners

At CROSSVIA, we value the strength of strategic partnerships to enhance our capabilities and broaden our impact, focusing on forging meaningful relationships with distinguished organizations that share our dedication to excellence in the sports industry. Our strategic partners include industry leaders, innovators, and key players across various sectors, each bringing unique expertise and perspectives to enhance our offerings. By combining our collective strengths, we deliver tailored solutions that drive success, enhance performance, and unlock new opportunities in the ever-evolving sports landscape. Explore our strategic partnerships and discover how our collaborative expertise can help you achieve a lasting impact within the sports industry.

Tour International

With over 30 years of expertise, Tour International is a leading provider of sports and educational travel experiences. It specializes in sports festivals, training camps, and educational tours and excels at creating, managing, and executing prestigious sporting events worldwide, ensuring seamless and luxurious experiences.

Great SportsTech

Great SportsTech Limited (GSL) is a leading provider of turnkey sports infrastructure solutions in South Asia, with over two decades of industry expertise. Specializing in designing, developing, and implementing state-of-the-art sports facilities, GSL delivers comprehensive, next-generation solutions that integrate the latest innovations in sports infrastructure.

Arka Alliance

Arka is a dynamic sports and esports marketing agency that excels in forging authentic, impactful partnerships for brands. With extensive expertise in both traditional sports and the rapidly growing esports industry, Arka delivers tailored marketing solutions designed to meet the distinct needs of each sector.

53Six

53six is a creative agency specializing in brand strategy, design, web development, and digital marketing. The agency helps businesses build strong brand identities, develop user-friendly websites, and implement impactful digital marketing and transformation campaigns through an integrated approach.

Vortigate

Vortigate is a leading provider of virtual reality (VR) and augmented reality (AR) services, specializing in creating interactive experiences that redefine digital reality. With a focus on innovation, Vortigate pioneers advanced solutions that push the boundaries of what is possible in the digital space.
